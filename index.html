About Me


Hey! I am a professional photographer who grew up in Nilgiris, India. As a child, I loved spending my days outdoors, playing and exploring. Serene mountain views have always been my escape. During my teen years, I discovered photography. I immediately fell in love with film photography, and now, it is my passion.

I love people watching. I like to capture images of simple life and slow living. I lurk around with my favourite camera and bring home memories immortalized in my amazing photographs.

Here, you could find a little of me and lot of my passion.
